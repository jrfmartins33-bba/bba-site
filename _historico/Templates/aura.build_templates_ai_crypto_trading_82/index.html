AI Crypto Trading Platform Landing Page Template

A dark, neon-accented landing page template inspired by MONSTER, built for AI-driven onchain trading and DeFi platforms.

What's Included

  • Hero section with bold headline, thesis copy, and dual CTAs
  • Multi-chapter narrative: Thesis, Execution Fabric, Intelligence, Ecosystem, Proof
  • Product highlights for multiple offerings (e.g., Pro venue, Arena exchange)
  • Partner/backs section and Labs capabilities grid
  • Futuristic footer with community and contact CTAs

Key Features

  • Futuristic dark UI with neon gradients and glassmorphism
  • Sections tailored to complex Web3 ecosystems and AI agents
  • Reusable cards for features, modules, and capabilities
  • Anchor-based navigation for long-form storytelling
  • CTA patterns for docs, ecosystem, community, and BD

Primary Use Case: Ideal for Web3, DeFi, and AI trading teams needing a brand-forward, narrative-driven landing page to explain complex onchain infrastructure and products.
